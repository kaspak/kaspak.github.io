About Me

I'm Kassy Pak and I'm currently a computer science major at the University of San Francisco. (Class of 2022!)

I am an adventurous developer with extensive experience leading teams and projects from concept to development. Through my leadership experience, I highly enjoy working with others and tailoring my stories through my growth and experiences. I enjoy teaching, learning, and I'm passionate about computer science education given my background experience of introducing and teaching K-8 kids how to code.

My main interests include: back-end, network programming, security, and empowering others to discover their superpower. One fact that anyone should know about me is that I love asking questions and creating engaging conversations that can probably go on for hours.

If you are interested in seeing what I've been working on, please check out my projects.
